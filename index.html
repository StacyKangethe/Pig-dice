Pig Dice

The game is played with a single six-sided dice. Roll dice as many times as you want and use the hold turn button to give your opponent a chance to play.
Game Rules:
1.The opponent that attains a total of 100 is 'The Winner'.
2.If you roll a ONE, you lose all the points you accumulated during that turn, and you lose your turn.
